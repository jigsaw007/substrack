Privacy Policy

Effective: January 2025

At substrackr, your privacy comes first. We collect only the information necessary to help you track your subscriptions — never more.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up, we store your email and any details you manually add about your subscriptions, such as names, prices, or renewal dates. We do not access your bank accounts or read external financial data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to generate and personalize your dashboard experience within substrackr. We do not share, sell, or display your data to any third party — ever.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your account. You may delete it at any time, and all associated data will be permanently erased from our system.

4. Cookies & Tracking

substrackr uses minimal cookies for secure login sessions and performance improvements only. We never use tracking or marketing cookies.

5. Your Rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
